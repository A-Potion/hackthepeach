Hack the Peach?

hack the Peach is a 24 hour hackathon welcoming all high school students, regardless of skill level

In a team of 4 you will create a project based on a theme we select together, and have a shot at winning prizes through peer-voting!

Knowing how to code is a valuable skills in today's world. Whether you have completed various projects before or have naver typed a line of code, Hack the Peach will help you foster and develop these skills.

FAQ

How much does it cost?

Hack the Peach is completely free. Food, drinks, workshops, prizes, and a place to sleep are all provided by us.

Who is this organized by?

Hack the Peach is organizied by local high school students but sponsored by the global non-profit Hack Club. The event while led by the organizers, will be supervised by adult chaperones.

What will we do?

During the 24 hours of hackathon participants will form teams of 3-4 to create a project according to the theme, which will be announced during the opening ceremony.

What should I bring?

We ask that you bring yourself, a sleeping bag, a laptop with a charger... and most importantly: a good spirit! For other items, please refer to the parent/attendee guide you'll receive in the coming weeks.
